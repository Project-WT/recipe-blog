बेसन का हलवा
Ingredients
  • ¼ cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 cup besan (gram flour or chickpea flour)
  • 1.5 cups of hot milk
  • ⅓ cup sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 4 to 5 pistachios , chopped
  • 4 to 5 almonds , chopped
  • few strands of saffron - optional
Cuisine Indian
Course Desserts
Prep time 30 mins
Servings 2/3
