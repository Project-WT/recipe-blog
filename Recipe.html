Crispy lotus stem in Honey Chilly.

A delicious snack time recipe, Crispy Lotus Stem in Honey Chilly is a very easy dish to prepare when you cannot think of anything else. Apart from its amazing taste, another great thing about this recipe is that it is a completely vegan dish. It is made with thin lotus stem slices. The slices are first dipped in ice-cold water to get rid of the excess starch and then blanched for 2 minutes in boiling water. The spices added to the dish are very basic and can be easily found in your local stores. You can always adjust the spice quotient of the dish. The honey added to it not only elevates that taste but also balances the spices and the salt. You can prepare Crispy Lotus Stem in Honey Chilly for your next party at home or even at a game night. To begin, follow our step-by-step recipe below! (Recipe: The Little Easy)

INGREDIENTS
  1. 10 grms ginger
  2. 3 tablespoons honey
  3. 1/2 teaspoon thai red chilli
  4. 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  5. 300 grams lotus stem
  6. 1/2 teaspoon sesame seeds
DIRECTIONS
  1. Step 1 Cut thin slices of the lotus stem
  2. To soften the stem slices, put them in boiling water and blanch for 2 minutes. Once done, keep aside to cool down.
  3. Step 2 Blend the chillis to make a paste
  4. Step 3 Fry the lotus stem slices and prepare the final dish
  5. Add some 2-3 tsp water to it and also add the honey
